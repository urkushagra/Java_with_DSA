## 📌 Real-Life Analogy
- **Encapsulation** → Heart is hidden, only accessed in a controlled way.  
- **Abstraction** → We know "Vehicle" exists, but each vehicle starts differently.  
- **Inheritance** → Children inherit traits from parents, just like Car/Bike inherit from Vehicle.  
- **Polymorphism** → "Start()" behaves differently for Car and Bike.
